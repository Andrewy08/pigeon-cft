# CFT Self-Compassion Diary — Installation Guide

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA) based on Module 16 of 
"CFT from the Inside Out" by Kolts, Bell, Bennett-Levy & Irons.

---

## How to put this on your Android phone (GitHub Pages)

### Step 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to https://github.com and click **Sign up**
2. Choose a username, enter your email, create a password
3. Verify your email when prompted

### Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** icon (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it exactly: `cft-diary`
3. Make sure it's set to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 — Upload the app files
1. On your new repository page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag and drop ALL files from this zip:
   - `index.html`
   - `manifest.json`
   - `sw.js`
   - The `icons/` folder (both icon files inside)
3. Scroll down and click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 — Turn on GitHub Pages
1. Click **Settings** (in your repository)
2. Click **Pages** (in the left sidebar)
3. Under "Source", select **Deploy from a branch**
4. Under "Branch", select **main** and click **Save**
5. Wait 1–2 minutes, then your app will be live at:
   `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/cft-diary`

### Step 5 — Install on your Android phone
1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Go to `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/cft-diary`
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right) → **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Add** — the app icon will appear on your home screen!

---

## Features
- 5-step CFT self-criticism diary (Module 16 structure)
- Soothing rhythm breathing animation
- Before/after belief and intensity ratings
- History of all entries
- Insights showing how ratings shift after the exercise
- Works fully offline after first load
- Data stays private on your phone

---

## Privacy
All your diary entries are stored **only on your phone** using the 
browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.
